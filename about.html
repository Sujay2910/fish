About Marine Life

Marine Life is a digital platform created to explore and educate about the fascinating creatures that inhabit our oceans. From tiny plankton to the majestic blue whale, our goal is to spread awareness about biodiversity and conservation.

This project showcases various marine species, their habitats, diets, and interesting facts to inspire ocean conservation among all.
